Happy Father's Day!

In Parkland's sunlit embrace you stay,
While I'm afar in Cincinnati's sway.
Though miles apart, my heart's display
Is celebrating with you on this Father's Day.

Wiggy, dear dad, with love profound,
I miss your laugh, your voice's sound.
But know, in spirit, I'm always around,
In every thought, in every joy we've found.

Today, Dad, let wine's mirth sway,
Indulge freely, it's your special day!
Toast to memories, both old and new,
For in my heart, I'm celebrating with you.

Happy Father's Day, from miles away,
In spirit, united, come what may.
To the best dad, with love that won't decay,
Here's to you, Antonio, on your special day!
